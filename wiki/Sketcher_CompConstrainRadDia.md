# Sketcher CompConstrainRadDia

## Description

**Constrain radius or diameter** is an icon button in the Sketcher constraints toolbar that groups tools to constrain the radius or diameter of an arc or a circle, or the weight of a B-spline pole. Click on the down arrow to its right to expand the icons below it and select a tool.

## Types of constraints 

-   <img alt="" src=images/Sketcher_ConstrainRadius.svg  style="width:32px;"> [Radius or weight](Sketcher_ConstrainRadius.md): Defines the radius of an arc or circle or the weight of a B-spline pole.

-   <img alt="" src=images/Sketcher_ConstrainDiameter.svg  style="width:32px;"> [Diameter](Sketcher_ConstrainDiameter.md): Defines the diameter of an arc or circle.

-   <img alt="" src=images/Sketcher_ConstrainRadiam.svg  style="width:32px;"> [Auto radius/diameter](Sketcher_ConstrainRadiam.md): Defines the radius of an arc, the diameter of a circle or the weight of a B-spline pole.
