## Introduzione

<img alt="Curves External workbench icon" src=images/Curves_workbench_icon.svg  style="width:128px;"> {{TOCright}}

The Curves workbench is an [External workbench](external_workbenches.md) that is python based with a collection of tools for NURBS curves and surfaces. This workbench is developed with FreeCAD Master and OCC 7.4. Note: Some tools may not work with earlier versions.

## Installation

Download the Curves workbench via the <img alt="" src=images/Std_AddonMgr.svg  style="width:24px;"> [Addon Manager](Std_AddonMgr.md)

## Tools

-   <img alt="" src=images/Curves_Line.svg  style="width:32px;"> [Curves Line](Curves_Line.md): Create a parametric line between two vertices.
-   <img alt="" src=images/Curves_EditableSpline.svg  style="width:32px;"> [Curves EditableSpline](Curves_EditableSpline.md): Create a freehand B-Spline curve.
-   <img alt="" src=images/Curves_ExtendCurve.svg  style="width:32px;"> [Curves ExtendCurve](Curves_ExtendCurve.md): Extends the selected edge.
-   <img alt="" src=images/Curves_JoinCurve.svg  style="width:32px;"> [Curves JoinCurve](Curves_JoinCurve.md): Joins the selected edges into BSpline Curves.
-   <img alt="" src=images/Curves_SplitCurve.svg  style="width:32px;"> [Curves SplitCurve](Curves_SplitCurve.md): Splits the selected edge.
-   <img alt="" src=images/Curves_Discretize.svg  style="width:32px;"> [Curves Discretize](Curves_Discretize.md): Discretize an edge or a wire.
-   <img alt="" src=images/Curves_Interpolate.svg  style="width:32px;"> [Curves Interpolate](Curves_Interpolate.md): Interpolate points with a BSpline curve.
-   <img alt="" src=images/Curves_ParametricComb.svg  style="width:32px;"> [Curves ParametricComb](Curves_ParametricComb.md): Creates a parametric Comb plot on selected edges.
-   <img alt="" src=images/Curves_ZebraTool.svg  style="width:32px;"> [Curves ZebraTool](Curves_ZebraTool.md): Zebra texture for surface inspection.
-   <img alt="" src=images/Curves_ParametricSolid.svg  style="width:32px;"> [Curves ParametricSolid](Curves_ParametricSolid.md): Make a parametric solid from selected faces.
-   <img alt="" src=images/Curves_IsoCurve.svg  style="width:32px;"> [Curves IsoCurve](Curves_IsoCurve.md): Create an IsoCurve from a face.
-   <img alt="" src=images/Curves_SketchOnSurface.svg  style="width:32px;"> [Curves SketchOnSurface](Curves_SketchOnSurface.md): Map a sketch on to a surface.
-   <img alt="" src=images/Curves_ProfileSupport.svg  style="width:32px;"> [Curves ProfileSupport](Curves_ProfileSupport.md): Creates a support plane for sketches.
-   <img alt="" src=images/Curves_PasteSVG.svg  style="width:32px;"> [Curves PasteSVG](Curves_PasteSVG.md): Pastes the SVG content of the clipboard.
-   <img alt="" src=images/Curves_PipeshellProfile.svg  style="width:32px;"> [Curves PipeshellProfile](Curves_PipeshellProfile.md): Creates a Profile object for PipeShell.
-   <img alt="" src=images/Curves_Pipeshell.svg  style="width:32px;"> [Curves Pipeshell](Curves_Pipeshell.md): Creates a Pipeshell sweep object.
-   <img alt="" src=images/Curves_GordonSurface.svg  style="width:32px;"> [Curves GordonSurface](Curves_GordonSurface.md): Creates a surface that skins a network of curves.
-   <img alt="" src=images/Curves_SegmentSurface.svg  style="width:32px;"> [Curves SegmentSurface](Curves_SegmentSurface.md): Segment a surface on isocurves.
-   <img alt="" src=images/Curves_ToConsole.svg  style="width:32px;"> [Curves ToConsole](Curves_ToConsole.md): Moves objects to the console.
-   <img alt="" src=images/Curves_CompressionSpring.svg  style="width:32px;"> [Curves CompressionSpring](Curves_CompressionSpring.md): Creates a compression spring.
-   <img alt="" src=images/Curves_ReflectLines.svg  style="width:32px;"> [Curves ReflectLines](Curves_ReflectLines.md): Creates the reflect lines on a shape, according to a view direction.
-   <img alt="" src=images/Curves_MultiLoft.svg  style="width:32px;"> [Curves MultiLoft](Curves_MultiLoft.md): Loft profile objects made of multiple faces in parallel.
