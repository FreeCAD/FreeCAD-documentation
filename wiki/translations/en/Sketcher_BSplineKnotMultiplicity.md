# Sketcher BSplineKnotMultiplicity/en

## Description

The <img alt="" src=images/Sketcher_BSplineKnotMultiplicity.svg  style="width:24px;"> [Sketcher BSplineKnotMultiplicity](Sketcher_BSplineKnotMultiplicity.md) tool shows or hides the [B-spline](B-Splines.md) knot multiplicity in all sketches.

<img alt="" src=images/Sketcher_KnotMultiplicity_multiplicity3.png  style="width:500px;"> 
*The numbers (1), (3) and (4) (green, in round brackets) refer to the [multiplicity](Sketcher_BSplineIncreaseKnotMultiplicity.md) of the knot points.*

## Usage

1.  There are several ways to invoke the tool:
    -   Press the **<img src="images/Sketcher_BSplineKnotMultiplicity.svg" width=16px> [B-spline knot multiplicity](Sketcher_BSplineKnotMultiplicity.md)** button.
    -   Select the **Sketch → Sketcher visual → Show/hide B-spline information layer → <img src="images/Sketcher_BSplineKnotMultiplicity.svg" width=16px> B-spline knot multiplicity** option from the menu.
