# Curves ReflectLines/en

## Description

The <img alt="" src=images/Curves_ReflectLines.svg  style="width:24px;"> [Curves ReflectLines](Curves_ReflectLines.md) creates the reflect lines on a shape, according to a view direction. This tool is part of the [external workbench](External_workbenches.md) called [Curves](Curves_Workbench.md).

## Usage

1.  Switch to the <img alt="" src=images/Curves_workbench_icon.svg  style="width:24px;"> _ is necessary, if not previously installed)
2.  To invoke the command, do one of the following:
    -   Press the <img alt="" src=images/Curves_ReflectLines.svg  style="width:24px;"> button in the Curves toolbar.
    -   Use the **Surfaces → Reflect Lines** entry in the dropdown menu.
