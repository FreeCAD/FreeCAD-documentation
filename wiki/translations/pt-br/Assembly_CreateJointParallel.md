## Descrição


<div lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr">

The <img alt="" src=images/Assembly_CreateJointParallel.svg  style="width:24px;"> [Assembly CreateJointParallel](Assembly_CreateJointParallel.md) tool creates a Parallel joint between two selected parts, setting the Z axes of selected coordinate systems parallel.


</div>

<div lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr">

1.  There are several ways to invoke the tool:

    -   Select the **Assembly → <img src="images/Assembly_CreateJointParallel.svg" width=16px> Create Parallel Joint** option from the menu.
    -   Use the keyboard shortcut: **N**.
